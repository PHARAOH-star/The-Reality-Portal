Morning Dew

June 12, 2023

The world awakes in silver light,
A fragile veil of morning bright.
Each blade of grass, a jeweled spear,
Adorned with drops of crystal clear.

The spider's web, a diamond lace,
Suspended in its airy space.
The rosebud holds a trembling sphere,
A liquid pearl, so perfect, dear.

Oh fleeting beauty, soon you'll rise,
Ascending to the waiting skies.
But in this moment, pure and true,
The earth is dressed in morning dew.
